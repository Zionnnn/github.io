Project idea.

Overview

My project is talking about food delivery app in mobile phone. With the continuous progress of the Internet society, people's lives have become more and more convenient. One of them is that people can place orders with one click through the food delivery app on their mobile phones, and then they can wait for the courier to deliver them at home. This type of APP can quickly deliver fresh and satisfying food to the door with convenient daily necessities, and truly achieve convenient services without leaving the house.

Motivation.

Food delivery apps have a broad user base all over the world. Especially teenagers and office workers between 15 and 25 years old. Now most parts of the world are plagued by COVID-19, so contactless delivery and the ability to solve food problems without leaving the house have made food delivery apps more demanding. Of course, this kind of distribution APP platform can not only distribute food, but also daily necessities, epidemic prevention supplies, and various tools that may be used in life. When the business expands, the distribution platform APP can also enter the distribution grocery market. The business scope of distribution APP is quite wide, it can become an indispensable part of people's life.

Description.

First of all, customers can browse the corresponding information of food materials or small products in life from the product information provided by merchants stationed on the platform. This information can provide users with a reference to measure the value of the product so that customers can judge whether they should buy the product. The second point is that the platform can launch “recommended today”. Customers can get products recommended by merchants like customers through “recommend today”, and help customers make a choice so that the platform can obtain higher sales. The third point is recipe recommendation. When customers choose their favourite foods or tools, the platform's system will automatically recommend products that are similar or related to the purchased products to customers, helping customers purchase combinations or recipes that match the products. The fourth point is that the food delivery platform supports multiple payment methods. Customers can purchase goods through online payment or cash, but in Australia's current situation, platforms usually use online payment methods, such as credit card and we chat pay or Alipay. The fifth point is that customers can purchase ingredients or other products online through the Internet, so that they can shop without leaving home. The sixth point is that the food delivery app will support the global positioning system. Customers can not only check the delivery status of the delivery staff on the platform, but also correctly know the geographic location of the merchants. The platform can also quickly recommend nearby businesses to customers through the positioning system. The seventh point is that the food delivery APP will require merchants to provide online appointment services. Customers can choose when the merchant will deliver the goods to ensure the freshness of the delivered ingredients. The eighth point is that the initial interface of the platform will have a news section. If customers are interested in news content, they can quickly find product information and purchase. This can increase product usage. The ninth point is that APP will have a lot of video teaching on the platform. If customers have this need, they can browse the video instruction provided by the merchant to get relevant knowledge on how to cook. The tenth point is that if the customer finds that the product does not match or is abnormal after receiving the product, he can choose to reject it and ask the platform or merchant to refund compensation.

Tools and Technologies.

UI design is one of the tools of the development platform. UI design is the overall interface design work of the user interface designed by the designer based on the prototype, the functional icons and content filling of the app. The second is technology development. It is divided into front-end Android development, front-end IOS development, back-end development, and database development. The third step is app testing. Strict testing is required after app development, including functional testing and interface stress testing.

Skills Required

UI designers need to use Stetch and XD in mobile apps and need to meet the correctness of the four elements of the plane; know how to use standard system Kits; can reasonably define fonts and element sizes; be familiar with and be able to design mainstream component types. Software development requires C++ or Java programming language and now the front-end has higher and higher requirements for algorithms, especially Coding capabilities. The company can accurately understand the requirements and quickly implement high-quality code. In addition, developers who master the basic knowledge of Web security, browser composition and principles, are proficient in using common front-end frameworks (React/Vue, etc.) and have a certain understanding of their principles will be more excellent.

Outcome

If this project is successful, people’s eating habits will change and a new option will be added to get food. The development of takeaway apps has promoted social and economic development and also saved time for various groups of people in society. The emergence of this life phenomenon shows people's pursuit of life diversity. The development of takeaway apps also provides employment opportunities for idle labor in society and drives the economic development of society. With the maturity of app software development technology, people have gradually become accustomed to using mobile app programs, which will also drive various industry apps for scheduled door-to-door services.
